The maker

Purple Clover Creations is run by a young crafter who fell in love with crochet and wanted to share the wiggly, squishy, snuggly results with the world.

Grown-ups help out with the grown-up stuff — answering messages, mailing parcels, and the tricky stitches. Everything soft and handmade comes straight from the heart. 🍀

Little questions

How do I actually order something?

Just say hello through the form above! Tell us what you're imagining and we'll chat colors, size & whether it's doable. Everything starts with a conversation.

How long does a custom piece take?

It really varies by size and detail. Some little friends are quick; big blankets take longer. We'll give you a realistic idea once we know the project. (exact timelines TBD)

How much do things cost?

Each piece is priced by hand based on yarn, size & stitchiness. We'll share a clear quote before we begin. (no fixed price list yet)

Do you ship?

We'd love to get your friend to you! Mailing details & areas are figured out per order with grown-up help. (shipping scope TBD)

Is everything really handmade?

Yes — every single loop. No machines, just hooks, yarn, patience, and the occasional tangled mess that becomes part of the charm.
